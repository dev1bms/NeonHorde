Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026 — Neon Horde for iOS

Neon Horde does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

  • No account or sign-in exists.
  • The app makes no network connections — it is fully playable offline.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.
  • Game progress (upgrades, best scores, settings) is saved only on your device and is deleted when you delete the app.
  • If you use the share feature, the generated image goes only where you choose to send it via the iOS share sheet. Saving it to Photos happens only when you tap "Save Image" and grants nothing to us.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing for us to sell, retain, or disclose — to anyone, ever.
